What Caused the Big Bang?

Published on CosmicHeist

Time seems to move in only one direction. We remember the past but not the future. Why does the universe behave this way?

Physicists believe the answer may lie in entropy — the tendency of systems to move toward disorder. As entropy increases, the universe evolves from ordered states into more probable chaotic ones.

This simple rule may be responsible for the direction of time itself.
